Odia is a language first written by Sarala Das, but has been used in other stories like the Mahabharata. It is an Eastern Indo-Aryan language from the Indo-Aryan family. It is thought to be descended from Prakrit, another language in the Indo-Aryan empire. Comparing against other languages in India, Odia seems to have little influence from the Persian and Arabic languages. The history of Odia is split up into 5 parts: Proto Odia, Early Middle Odia, Middle Odia, Late Middle Odia, and Modern Odia. There is about 55 million people across the globe who speak Odia.
